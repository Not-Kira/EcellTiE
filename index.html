TiE Grad Kick-off Session

The morning session will be a TiE Grad Kick-off Session with MoU Exchange followed by a Session of Idea Generation and Validation after lunch.It is an Entrepreneurship Talk to consolidate the concepts and foundations of entrepreneurship among our youth and to create awareness on modern entrepreneurship approaches and to transform their projects to Economic Reality.

TiE

The IndUS Entrepreneurs (TiE) is a Silicon Valley non-profit supporting start-ups through networking and the raising of capital for new and experienced entrepreneurs.By 2000, it had helped create businesses worth more than $75 billion.

ECell

NIET eCell is a students run organization which promotes entrepreneurship among students fraternity of NIET and other aspiring student entrepreneurs of the region, state and nation. Run and governed by a Managing Committee and managed by a student executive team under supervision of the NIET Technology Business Incubator.
